About Me

Welcome! My name is Rebecca Lyn Renavitz and I am a Rutgers Coding Bootcamp graduate and web developer. I received my Bachelor of Arts in Anthropology from University of Delaware in 2007 and completed Master's coursework in Epidemiology at The State University of New York-University at Albany. I became interested in web development after working on a project to revamp my current company's website. It was a lot of fun, so I experimented on my own and...here I am!


Web development is exciting because it requires analytical skill as well as creativity. I am detail-oriented, logical, and creative. I truly enjoy using the knowledge I have and applying it to solving problems...and building apps! Cheers!
